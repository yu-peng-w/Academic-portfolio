## About Me

I am a PhD researcher at King's College London, working on the miniaturisation of vision-based tactile sensors and their medical and surgical applications. My research is supervised by [Professor Shan Luo](https://shanluo.github.io/), with [Dr Lukas Lindenroth](https://www.kcl.ac.uk/people/lukas-lindenroth) as my secondary supervisor. My research interests include vision-based tactile sensing, surgical robotics, medical devices, and robot perception.

I received my Bachelor of Engineering in Mechatronic Engineering from the University of New South Wales (UNSW) in 2023.

In my spare time, I enjoy watching sports, particularly football and Formula 1. I am also a photography enthusiast with a particular interest in film photography and darkroom printing.
